## SLIDE 11: DETRACTOR PAIN #1 - TRANSFER FAILURES

**Title:** Pain Category #1: Transfer Failures & Instability

**Percentage:** 38.8% of detractor complaints

**What Users Experienced:**
Transfers that failed silently, repeated attempts without success, app freezes during transactions, uncertainty about whether money was actually sent. Many users report trying 3-5+ times.

**User Quotes:**
- "I've tried more than 5 times to transfer money and I can't" ‚≠ê1
- "Sometimes it takes a while, it's not fast and you don't know if the transfer was made or not" ‚≠ê4

**UX Impact:**
Users don't just lose the transaction ‚Äî they lose confidence in using Nu for any financial operation. Several verbatims mention considering switching to other banks.

---

## SLIDE 12: DETRACTOR PAIN #2 - ACCOUNT BLOCKS

**Title:** Pain Category #2: Account Blocks & No Resolution

**Percentage:** ~5% of detractor complaints

**What Users Experienced:**
Persistent blocks, particularly for Nu ‚Üî Nequi transfers, that haven't been resolved despite reaching out to support. Users feel stuck and ignored.

**User Quotes:**
- "There's a persistent block on my Nu account against my Nequi account and they don't give me a solution" ‚≠ê1
- "How is it possible that I can't pay taxes with this account?? And I can't make large movements either" ‚≠ê2

**UX Impact:**
Creates a feeling of being "trapped" ‚Äî they have money in Nu but can't send it where they need. Especially frustrating for users who relied on Nu as their primary account.

---

## SLIDE 13: DETRACTOR PAIN #3 - 4x1000 CONFUSION

**Title:** Pain Category #3: 4x1000 Confusion & Frustration

**Percentage:** ~15% of detractor complaints

**What Users Experienced:**
Users feel "surprised" by 4x1000 deductions. They can't track accumulated tax, don't understand exemption rules, and question charges they believe shouldn't apply.

**User Quotes:**
- "Every time I make a transfer, regardless of the amount, 4x1000 is deducted" ‚≠ê1
- "According to a decree, 4x1000 shouldn't be charged if you don't exceed an amount, but they're deducting it from me" ‚≠ê3

**UX Impact:**
Creates perception that Nu is "taking extra money" or not being transparent. Some users avoid Nu for large transfers specifically because of 4x1000 uncertainty.

---

## SLIDE 14: DETRACTOR PAIN #4 - PROCESS TOO LONG

**Title:** Pain Category #4: Process Too Long & Confusing

**Percentage:** ~10% of detractor complaints

**What Users Experienced:**
Too many taps to complete a transfer, difficulty finding where contacts are saved, confusing navigation after completing a transfer.

**User Quotes:**
- "It's too long to make the payment, too many steps to pay" ‚≠ê2

## SLIDE 15: KEY DETRACTOR INSIGHT

**Title:** The Common Thread Among Detractors

**Main Insight (large text):**
Detractors feel a **LOSS OF CONTROL**

**Explanation:**
Whether it's a failed transfer, an unexplained block, a surprise tax deduction, or a confusing flow ‚Äî they don't understand what's happening and can't fix it themselves.
